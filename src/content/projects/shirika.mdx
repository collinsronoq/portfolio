## Overview
Shirika helps communities track monthly contributions, balances, and spending transparently.

## Problem
Many community/estate systems struggle with:
- Manual records and inconsistencies
- Disputes due to weak audit trails
- Lack of clear balances per household/member

## Solution
- Contribution tracking per household/unit
- Expenditure recording + receipts/reporting
- Role-based access (admin vs user)
- Dashboard summaries for quick decisions

## Architecture Notes
- FastAPI routes organized by domain modules
- SQLAlchemy models structured for reporting and balances
- Authentication and authorization layered cleanly

## Learnings
- Data modeling for balances and monthly contributions
- Designing admin workflows for real usage
